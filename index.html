Financial Formula

Present value of cash flows (discounted cash flow):

\( \displaystyle \text{PV} = \sum_{t=1}^{T} \frac{CF_t}{(1 + r)^t} \)

Where \(CF_t\) = cash flow at time t, \(r\) = discount rate.
